The Event
Built for the Bold
HIRAETH 2026 is a two-day motorsport and culture event spanning two distinct venues — a live MX race arena and a curated expo yard. Patronised by HPTDC (Himachal Government Tourism Wing), inaugurated by Cabinet ministers, and built for a generation that rides, creates, and competes.
